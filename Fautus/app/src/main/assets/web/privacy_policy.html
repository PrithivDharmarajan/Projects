Fautus Privacy Policy

At Fautus, we want to connect people and bring communities together. In this privacy policy, we tell you what information we receive from Fautus Consumers and Photographers, and how we use it to connect Consumers with Photographers and continue to improve our services. Below, we explain how you can share with other Consumers and Photographers in the Fautus community as part of our mission to bring people together.

1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

Fautus (“Fautus,” “we,” “our,” and/or “us”) values the privacy of individuals who use our application, websites, and related services (collectively, the “Fautus Platform ”). This privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy”) explains how we collect, use, and share information from Fautus users (“Users”), comprised of both Fautus Consumers (“Consumers”) and Fautus Photographers (including Photographer applicants) (“Photographers”). Beyond the Privacy Policy, your use of Fautus is also subject to our Terms of Service ( www.Fautus.com/terms).

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

Registration Information. When you sign up for a Fautus account, you give us your name, email address, and some times your phone number and a profile photo.

User Profile Information. When you join the Fautus community, you can create a Fautus Profile to share fun facts about yourself, and discover mutual friends and interests. Filling out a profile is optional, and you can share as little or as much as you want. Your name (and for Photographers, Profile photos) is always part of your Profile. Read more below about how you can control who sees your Profile.

Payment Method. When you add a credit card or payment method to your Fautus account, a third party that handles payments for us will receive your card information. To keep your financial data secure, we do not store full credit card information on our servers.

Communications. If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you. For example, when you contact our Customer Support Team, we will receive your name, email address, phone number, the contents of a message or attachments that you may send to us, and other information you choose to provide.

Photographer Application Information. If you decide to join our Fautus Photographer community, in addition to the basic registration information we ask you for your date of birth, physical address, mailing address, if different, Social Security number, a photo of a valid government issued ID and a selfie to match with your ID. We share this information with our partners who help us by running background checks on Photographers to help protect the Fautus community.

Payment Information. To make sure Photographers get paid, we partner with the best in the market, Stripe, Inc. Stripe, Inc., is a U.S. Technology company operating in about 25 different countries that allows both, private individuals and businesses to accept payment over the internet and in mobile applications. We do not keep information about Photographers’ bank routing numbers, tax information, social security numbers or any other payment information provided by Photographers. When you provide such sensitive information you are providing it directly to Stripe Inc. When you are creating a profile with Fautus you agree to Stripe, Inc’s own Terms of Service in addition to Fautus Terms of Service.

B. Information We Collect When You Use the Fautus Platform

Location Information. Fautus is all about connecting Photographers and Consumers. To do this, we need to know where you are. When you open Fautus on your mobile device, we receive your location. We may also collect the precise location of your device when the app is running in the foreground or background. If you label certain locations, such as “home” and “work,” we receive that information, too.

Your location information is necessary for things like matching Consumers with nearby Photographers, determining Moment locations, and suggesting nearby photo opportunity locations. Also, if the need ever arises, we may use and share location information to help protect the safety of Fautus Users or a member of the public. If you give us permission through your device settings or Fautus app, we may collect your location while the app is off to identify promotions or service updates in your area.

Device Information. Fautus receives information from Users’ devices, including IP address, web browser type, mobile operating system version, phone carrier and manufacturer, application installations, device identifiers, mobile advertising identifiers, push notification tokens. We collect mobile sensor data from Photographers’ devices (such as camera band, number of photos) to improve location accuracy and quality of service.

Usage Information. To help us understand how you use the Fautus Platform and to help us improve it, we automatically receive information about your interactions with the Fautus Platform, like the pages or other content you view, your actions within the Fautus app, and the dates and times of your visits.

Call and Text Information. We work with a third party partner to facilitate phone calls and text messages between Consumers and Photographers who have been matched or have started or a Service. We receive information about these communications including the date and time of the call or SMS message, the parties’ phone numbers, and the content of any SMS messages. For security purposes, we may also monitor and/or record the contents of phone calls or text messages made on the Fautus Platform, such as those between Consumers and Photographers. You will be given notice that your call may be recorded, and by proceeding you agree to allow Fautus to monitor and/or record your call.

User Feedback. At Fautus, we want to make sure Users are always enjoying great experiences. Consumers and Photographers may rate and review each other at the end of every Moment. We receive information about ratings and reviews and, as we explain below, give Consumers information about Photographers’ ratings and reviews and vice versa.

Address Book Contacts. If you permit Fautus to access the address book on your device through the permission system used by your mobile platform, we may access and store names and contact information from your address book to facilitate invitations and social interactions that you initiate through our Platform and for other purposes described in this privacy policy or at the time of consent or collection.

Camera Roll. If you permit Fautus to access your Camera Roll on your device through the permission system used by your mobile platform, we may access the content to facilitate the use of the Fautus Platform and for other purposes described in this privacy policy or at the time of consent or collection.

Information from Cookies and Similar Technologies. We collect information through the use of “cookies”, tracking pixels, and similar technologies to understand how you navigate through the Fautus Platform and interact with Fautus advertisements, to learn what content is popular, and to save your preferences. Cookies are small text files that web servers place on your device; they are designed to store basic information and to help websites and apps recognize your browser. We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be accessed every time you use the Fautus Platform. You should consult your web browser(s) to modify your cookie settings. Please note that if you delete or choose not to accept cookies from us, you may be missing out on certain features of the Fautus Platform.

C. Information We Collect from Third Parties

Third Party Partners. We may receive additional information about you, such as demographic data, payment information, or fraud detection information, from third party partners and combine it with other information that we have about you.

Background Information on Photographers. Fautus works with third party partners to perform criminal background checks on Photographers, and we receive information from them such as publicly available.

3. How We Use the Information We Collect

We use the information we collect from all Users to:

  • Connect Consumers with Photographers;
  • Provide, improve, expand, and promote the Fautus Platform;
  • Analyze how the Fautus community uses the Fautus Platform;
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for marketing and promotional purposes;
  • Personalize the Fautus experience for you and your friends and contacts;
  • Send you text messages and push notifications;
  • Facilitate transactions and payments;
  • Provide you with customer support;
  • Find and prevent fraud; and
  • Respond to trust and safety issues that may arise, including incidents, disputes between Consumers and Photographers, and requests from government authorities.

Additionally, we use the information we collect from Photographers for the following purposes related to providing Services on the Fautus Platform:

  • Sending emails and text messages to Photographers who have started the Photographer application process regarding the status of their application;
  • Determining a Photographer’s eligibility to provide Services for Fautus
  • Notifying Photographers about Moment request, pricing and service updates; and

4. How We Share the Information We Collect

A. Sharing Between Users

Sharing between Consumers and Photographers. Consumers and Photographers that have been matched for a Moment or start a Snap Moment or related Services are able to see basic information about each other, such as names, photo, ratings, and any information they have added to their Profiles. Photographers see the location that the Consumer has provided. Consumers see a Photographer’s information and real-time location as the Photographer approaches the Consumers’ location. In some instances, Consumers’ ratings of Photographers are shared with Photographers on a weekly basis. We de-identify the ratings and feedback, but we can’t rule out that a Photographer may be able to identify the Consumer that provided the rating or feedback.

Although we help Consumers and Photographers communicate with one another to arrange a Service, we do not share your actual phone number or other contact information with other Users.

Sharing between Fautus Consumers. If you use Fautus Share feature, Consumers who have been matched with you will be able to see your name, photo and any information you have added to your Profile.

B. Sharing Between Fautus and Third Parties

API and Integration Partners. If you connect to the Fautus Platform through an integration with a third party service, we may share information about your use of the Fautus Platform with that third party. We may share your information with our third party partners in order to receive additional information about you. We may also share your information with third party partners to create offers that may be of interest to you. Please note our payment and billing service; in app messaging service; customer service, location services and emailing services are all API and Integration Partners.

Third Party Services. The Fautus Platform may allow you to connect with other websites, products, or services that we don’t have control over. If you use these services, we will provide the third party with information about you to allow them to provide the service to you. We can’t speak to the privacy practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies before deciding whether to use their services.

Service Providers. We work with third party service providers to perform services on our behalf, and we may share your information with such service providers to help us provide the Fautus Platform, including all of the things described in Section 3 above.

Other Sharing. We may share your information with third parties in the following cases:

  • While negotiating or in relation to a change of corporate control such as a restructuring, merger or sale of our assets;
  • If a government authority requests information and we think disclosure is required or appropriate in order to comply with laws, regulations, or a legal process;
  • With law enforcement officials, government authorities, or third parties if we think doing so is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Fautus community, Fautus, or the public;
  • To comply with a legal requirement or process, including but not limited to, civil and criminal subpoenas, court orders or other compulsory disclosures.
  • If you signed up for a promotion with another User’s referral or promotion code, with your referrer to let them know about your redemption of or qualification for the promotion;
  • With our insurance partners to help determine and provide relevant coverage in the event of an incident;
  • To provide information about the use of the Fautus Platform to potential business partners in aggregated or de-identified form that can’t reasonably be used to identify you; and
  • Whenever you consent to the sharing.

5. Your Choices

Email Subscriptions. You can always unsubscribe from our commercial or promotional emails but we will still send you transactional and relational emails about your account use of the Fautus Platform.

Push Notifications. You can opt out of receiving push notifications through your device settings. Please note that opting out of receiving push notifications may impact your use of the Fautus Platform (such as receiving a notification that your Photographer is nearby).

Profile Information. While your name will always be shared with Photographers and other Consumers you decide to share your moments or Services, you can delete any additional information that you added to your Profile at any time if you don’t want Photographers and Consumers to see it. Consumers will always be able to see Photographers’ names, rating, and profile photos.

Location Information. While you can prevent your device from sharing location information at any time through your Device’s operating system settings, Consumer and Photographer location is core to the Fautus Platform and without it we can’t provide our services to you.

Editing and Accessing Your Information. You can review and edit certain account information by logging in to your account settings and profile (Photographers may edit additional information through the Photographer portal). If you would like to terminate your Fautus account, please contact us through our Help Center with your request. If you choose to terminate your account, we will deactivate it for you but may retain information from your account for a certain period of time and disclose it in a manner consistent with our practices under this Privacy Policy for accounts that are not closed. We also may retain information from your account to collect any fees owed, resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, analyze usage of the Fautus Platform, assist with any investigations, prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, or take other actions as required or permitted by law.

6. Other

Data Security. We are committed to protecting the data of the Fautus community. Even though we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no security measures can be 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

Children’s Privacy. Fautus is not directed to children, and we don’t knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we find out that a child under 13 has given us personal information, we will take steps to delete that information. If you believe that a child under the age of 13 has given us personal information, please contact us at our Help Center.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy. We may make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will let you know through the Fautus Platform, by email, or other communication. We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy periodically to stay up-to-date about our privacy practices. As long as you use the Fautus Platform, you are agreeing to this Privacy Policy and any updates we make to it.

Contact Information. Feel free to contact us at any time with any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, your personal information, our use and sharing practices, or your consent choices by contacting our Help Center.
